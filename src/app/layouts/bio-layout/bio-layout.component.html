Definition of a Sheetz

Above all else, Sheetz is about providing kicked-up convenience while being more than just a convenience store. Sheetz is a mecca for people on the go. If you need to refuel your car or refresh your body, we have what you need to keep you moving on to whatever comes next.

Road warriors. Construction workers. Soccer moms. They all have a special place at Sheetz. We know you don't have time to run all over town for the things you need. Sheetz has what you need, when you need it. And, we're here 24/7/365. Even on Christmas day.

Committed to Our Customers

We really care about our customers. Our mission at Sheetz is to provide fast, friendly service and quality products in clean and convenient locations. We work hard to make sure we deliver on our Mission promise every day.

We are able to succeed because we have outstanding employees with a commitment to Total Customer Focus (TCF). Our employees strive to meet the needs of our busy customers. Our employees are also active participants in the communities in which we operate convenience stores.

Committed to Our Communities

We connect with our neighbors in a number of ways and through various charitable organizations like Special Olympics, youth sports groups and Sheetz For the Kidz®.

Sheetz employees started Sheetz For the Kidz® in 1992. Since then, we have raised millions of dollars to buy toys, clothes and other necessities for needy children during the holiday season. It's a charity that is very near and dear to our hearts, and our employees work hard to raise more money and help even more children each year.

Committed to an Inclusive and Equitable Culture

Sheetz has promised to create a more inclusive and equitable culture that benefits our stores, employees, and our communities. We know that honoring and recognizing our employees’ and customers’ diverse experiences, individual identities and unique perspectives makes us a stronger organization and partner in every community we serve.

The Sheetz family, our executives and leadership across the organization are working to identify actions that will lead to real progress. To that point, Sheetz has formally incorporated Inclusion, Diversity, Equity and Accessibility (IDEA) into our corporate strategy. This work includes tactical focus on resourcing, planning and education. Over the past year, we have made substantial progress toward educating our organization’s leadership teams and making them aware of topics associated with IDEA. We are also working with independent experts, discovering how we can translate this work to our employees, customers, and suppliers – and to create real change across the company. Part of that change is supporting organizations making a difference in our communities. Most recently, Sheetz pledged $100,000 in support of Embrace Race, an organization providing educational resources to parents in order to talk to their kids about race. You can read more about our major partners in this effort, here.

We know that this is just a start. That’s why we are committed to an ongoing assessment of our culture and values to ensure that all employees and customers feel a sense of belonging at Sheetz. We are also pledging to increase diverse representation across our organization, through talent development, talent acquisition and promotions, making sure that all employees have the chance to thrive. We are stronger together when everyone feels seen and included.

To maintain our accountability and to keep our communities updated on our progress we will continue to post here. We know that this work will only serve to strengthen our community connections and make Sheetz a better place for everyone.
